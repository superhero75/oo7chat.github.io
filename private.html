Privacy Policy

This document describes how we, as OO7 chat, collect your personal
information, use and share it.

When you register, we collect email address, firstname, lastname and company information like name and address.
On credit card registration, our payment processor collects your card information, IP and browser information. We do not get access to your full credit card number and do not store it. We keep only basic information like credit card owner name, 4 last digits, date of expiration or credit card type.
Your data are used internally to run the service and improve it.
Basically we collect and use only data we need.
We sometimes share some data with third party companies, but only for the purpose of using the tools they provide, such as our messenger tool or for statistical analysis.
We are attached to privacy so we never sell your data to third-party.
European residents benefit from a European protection of their data known as GDPR.
As these rules seem to us to be a step in the right direction with regard to privacy, we have decided to apply to all users of our services, even non-residents of the European Union.
So, whoever you are, European resident or not, you can get access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal information at any time (see the "Contact Us" section).
